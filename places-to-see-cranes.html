Places to see Cranes

Izumi Crane Observation Center

Kagoshima, Japan

Located towards the southern end of Japan, Izumi attracts over 10,000 migrating cranes of five species. On a good winter day, one can find Demoiselle Crane, White-naped Crane, Common Crane, Hooded Crane, and possibly Sandhill Crane.

Quivira National Wildlife Refuge

Kansas, United States

Located in central Kansas, Quivira National Wildlife Refuge attracts thousands of wintering Sandhill Cranes and several migrating Whooping Cranes each year. Peak season is late October and early November when one can possibly see a dozen Whooping Cranes stooping over before they continue their journey towards the Texas coast.

Poyang Lake

Jiangxi, China

Located in the northern Jiangxi province in China, Poyang lake is the largest freshwater lake in China and serves as wintering grounds for one of the rarest and most endangered cranes: the Siberian Crane.
