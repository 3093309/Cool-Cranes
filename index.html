Welcome to the world of cranes! There are 15 species worldwide from the critically endangered Siberian Crane to the widespread Sandhill Crane. Cranes are found on all continents except Antarctica and South America. In east Asian cultures cranes are considered sacred. Today, cranes face many threats, such as habitat loss, and many are threatened with extinction.
